How to Order

No accounts, no payment gateways — just eight simple steps between browsing and your order being on its way.

1

Browse products

Explore our catalog of 150+ products across 12 categories. Use search, filters, and sorting to find exactly what you need.

2

Open the product details page

Check specs, features, pricing, delivery time, and real customer reviews before deciding.

3

Click Buy Now

Choose your quantity and head to the order form — no login required.

4

Fill in your delivery information

Enter your name, mobile number, and complete delivery address. Everything is validated in real time so you don't miss a field.

5

Generate and download the PDF

We instantly generate a professional order sheet with your Order ID, invoice number, product details, and total — ready to download.

6

Send the PDF to our Instagram account

Open Instagram directly from the success page and DM the PDF to @mech_designhub.

7

Wait for our team to verify your order

A real person checks your details, product availability, and delivery location — usually within a few hours.

8

Receive confirmation and shipping updates

Once confirmed, we'll message you with next steps and keep you posted until your order arrives.
