Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Nuvora ("we," "us," "our") handles information in connection with this website. Nuvora is a frontend-only demo storefront — it has no backend server, database, or login system. Please read this policy alongside our Terms & Conditions.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Buy Now order form, you may enter the following information directly into your browser:

  • Full name, mobile number, and optionally your email address
  • Delivery address details, including house/flat number, street, area, landmark, city, district, state, PIN code, and country
  • Optional order notes or delivery instructions

This information is used only to generate a PDF order sheet inside your own browser. It is never transmitted to us automatically.

2. How We Use Information

Because this site has no backend, we do not receive any information you type into the order form unless you personally choose to send us the generated PDF via Instagram Direct Message. Only at that point do we see your details, and only to process and confirm your order.

3. Data Storage

Wishlist items and recently viewed products are stored locally in your browser using local storage, purely so those features work between visits. This data stays on your device and is not accessible to us. Order form data is not saved anywhere after you close or refresh the page, other than inside the PDF you choose to download.

4. Instagram Orders

If you send us your order PDF via Instagram, that exchange is governed by Instagram's own privacy policy and terms, in addition to this one. We recommend reviewing Instagram's policies as well.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

This site does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics. It uses browser local storage only for wishlist and recently-viewed functionality, as described above.

6. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Since we have no server, there is no database of customer information for us to share in the first place.

7. Your Rights

You may clear your wishlist and recently-viewed data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site. If you've sent us an order PDF via Instagram and would like it deleted from our conversation history, message us and we'll action that request.

8. Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@nuvora.shop or via Instagram DM to @mech_designhub.
